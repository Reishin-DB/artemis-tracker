## Data Sources

- Spacecraft telemetry data is sourced from the publicly available [JPL Horizons API](https://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/horizons/)
- Spacecraft specifications are from publicly published NASA documents
- Crew schedule information is from publicly available NASA mission agendas
- Mission milestones are based on publicly announced mission plans

## Accuracy

While this project uses real NASA data sources, it is a personal project and may contain:
- Delays in data freshness (data is polled every 5 minutes)
- Approximations in computed fields (distances, speeds)
- Simplified representations of spacecraft systems
- Mission timeline information that may not reflect real-time operational changes

**Do not use this application as a source of truth for the Artemis II mission.** For official mission information, visit [nasa.gov/artemis-ii](https://www.nasa.gov/mission/artemis-ii/).
